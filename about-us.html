About Us

Who We Are

We're a small group of enthusiastic Pokémon fans and web developers who decided to blend our love for coding with our love for catching 'em all. PokéFinder was born as a fun side project — part passion, part practice, and 100% made with love.

What Is PokéFinder?

PokéFinder is a fan-made web app that lets you search and explore Pokémon info in a quick, user-friendly way. Whether you're looking up your favorite starter or researching types before your next gym battle, we've got your back.

Why We Made This

This project was built to help us improve our web development skills while creating something we genuinely enjoy. PokéFinder isn’t official or commercial — it’s just a fun tool for fellow trainers who want a lightweight way to find Pokémon data.

Disclaimer

PokéFinder is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Nintendo, Game Freak, or The Pokémon Company. All Pokémon names, images, and data belong to their respective owners. We’re just fans, building cool stuff for fun.

Contact

Want to say hi or have a suggestion for PokéFinder? Reach out to our (fictional but friendly) team lead Ash at ash@pokefinder.dev. We’d love to hear from you — even if it’s just to debate the best starter.

Join the Adventure

Thanks for checking out PokéFinder. Whether you’re a rookie trainer or a seasoned Master, we hope this site adds a bit of joy to your journey. Now get out there and start exploring — the Pokédex won’t complete itself!
